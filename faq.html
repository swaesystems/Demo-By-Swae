Frequently Asked Questions

Get the answers you need

How long do Botox and filler results last?
Botox typically lasts 3–4 months, while dermal fillers can last anywhere from 6 months to over a year depending on the area treated and the product used.
Is IV therapy safe?
Yes, our IV drips are administered by trained professionals using sterile techniques. Each formulation is customized to your wellness goals.
Will I experience downtime after treatments?
Most of our treatments require little to no downtime. You may experience minor swelling or redness, but this typically resolves quickly.
How do I choose the right treatment?
During your consultation, we’ll assess your goals and skin condition to recommend the most effective treatments tailored to you.
