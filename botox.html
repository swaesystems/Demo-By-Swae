About Botox & Fillers

Botox and dermal fillers are two of the most popular non-surgical cosmetic treatments designed to reduce signs of aging and rejuvenate your facial appearance. Botox works by relaxing the facial muscles responsible for fine lines and wrinkles, while fillers restore lost volume and contour the face for a smoother, more youthful look.

At Luma Aesthetics, our certified specialists tailor each treatment to your unique facial structure and goals. Whether you're softening forehead lines, enhancing lips, or lifting your cheeks, our gentle and precise approach ensures natural-looking results that enhance your beauty and confidence.
