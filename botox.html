About Botox & Fillers

Botox and dermal fillers are two of the most effective non-surgical aesthetic treatments available today. At Luma Aesthetics, we use premium-grade injectables to help you achieve a refreshed, rejuvenated appearance with minimal downtime.

Botox works by relaxing facial muscles that cause expression lines, smoothing out wrinkles and preventing future ones from forming. Dermal fillers restore volume and enhance facial contours—perfect for softening smile lines, plumping lips, or defining cheekbones.

Our expert team ensures every treatment is tailored to your unique facial structure and aesthetic goals, offering natural-looking results that enhance—not change—your beauty.
