About Botox & Fillers

Botox and dermal fillers are non-surgical cosmetic treatments designed to rejuvenate your appearance. Botox is a purified protein that relaxes facial muscles to reduce fine lines and wrinkles — especially around the eyes and forehead. Dermal fillers, on the other hand, add volume and smoothness to areas like the cheeks, lips, and nasolabial folds.

At Luma Aesthetics, we tailor every treatment to enhance your natural beauty and ensure subtle, stunning results. Whether you’re looking to achieve a youthful glow, smooth deep wrinkles, or restore volume loss, our licensed specialists use the latest techniques to deliver safe, precise, and luxurious results.
