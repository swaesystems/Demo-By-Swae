About Botox & Fillers

Botox and dermal fillers are two of the most popular non-surgical aesthetic treatments available today. Botox helps to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles by temporarily relaxing targeted facial muscles. Dermal fillers, on the other hand, add volume and smoothness to areas such as the cheeks, lips, and under the eyes for a rejuvenated, youthful appearance.
