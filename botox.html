What to Expect

Rediscover youthful elegance with our signature Botox & Dermal Fillers — the gold standard in non-surgical facial rejuvenation. At Luma Aesthetics, our expert practitioners use advanced techniques to smooth dynamic wrinkles, lift tired features, and restore volume where time has taken its toll. Each treatment is customized to your facial anatomy and beauty goals, ensuring results that are subtle, balanced, and effortlessly radiant.

Botox works by gently relaxing targeted muscles that cause fine lines and expression wrinkles — most commonly around the eyes, forehead, and brow. The result is a refreshed, smooth appearance that never looks frozen. Dermal fillers complement Botox by restoring lost volume in areas like the cheeks, lips, and under-eyes — helping sculpt youthful contours and enhance your natural beauty.

Treatments are quick, virtually painless, and require minimal downtime. Most clients return to their day immediately, noticing visible improvements within days and full results in about two weeks. Whether you're aiming for prevention or enhancement, our approach is always refined, safe, and artistically driven.
